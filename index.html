About Us

Opened in 2011. Located at Woodbine and Danforth, Toronto. We believe in good food: food that is made proudly from scratch, using honest ingredients produced by folks that are getting paid for their skills and labour. We believe in supporting our local economy by sourcing from area farmers and vendors whenever possible. Our flours, grains, milk, cream, butter and eggs are all sourced locally.
